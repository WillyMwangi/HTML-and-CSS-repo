Life is made of three types of people. Those What make things happen,those who follow what happens and those who never understand what happens. I will tell you which type I am. However, I have learnt some important lesson but first I ask a questionhat differentiates extraordinary people from the ordinary ones? That “extra” is all it takes. However, I had support and encouragement from my fellow boot campers and my facilitator. The tasks to complete by the end of day were not a walk in the park..

I will be around this planet called earth for a short while. My days are numbered but I make the best use of every opportunity that come my way. God created me special, intelligent and awesome. It will be unfair if I hide it from people.
